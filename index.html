The Pomodoro Technique

What is it?

A time management method that uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes long, separated by short breaks.

How it Works

Work for 25 minutes (one Pomodoro), then take a 5-minute break. After four Pomodoros, take a longer 15-minute break.

Core Benefits

  • Maintains laser focus
  • Reduces mental fatigue
  • Sustains consistent productivity
